Fun Facts about Biryani

Who brought biryani to hyderabad?

The world famous Hyderabadi biryani came into being after Emperor Aurangzeb appointed Niza-Ul-Mulk as the new ruler of Hyderabad. Biryani was part of the menu for the troops under Niza-Ul-Mulk.While most other biryanis were dominated by their flavoured meat, in the layered Hyderabadi biryani, the aromatic saffron flavoured rice became the star of dish.
